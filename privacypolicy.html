Bible Atlas ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Bible Atlas Ministries LLC collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Bible Atlas mobile application.

By using the Bible Atlas app, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. About Bible Atlas

Bible Atlas is an app designed to help you explore and connect with the Bible and Christianity on a deeper level. As you read, you unlock characters and locations, bringing the stories and their meanings to life.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email address (used to create and manage your account)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

You can delete your account at any time within the app. This will permanently delete all associated user data from our systems.

Because email is used as a unique identifier for accounts, data collection is required for app usage, and there is no opt-out option at this time.
