Discover Characters

Unlock key figures as you read — from prophets and kings to everyday believers — and see how their stories connect to the bigger picture of Scripture.

Discover Locations

Unlock locations while you read. Bring context to the Word by uncovering the real-world settings of Scripture. From Bethlehem to Babylon, each location adds depth to your journey.

AI Bible Study Helper

Ask questions and dig deeper into Scripture with the Bible Study Helper. While it's a powerful tool, true understanding comes through prayer and the guidance of the Holy Spirit.

Save, Explain & Reflect on Any Verse

Highlight any verse to unlock a suite of tools: save it to your favorites, see a clear explanation, or add a personal note. Whether you're studying, praying, or journaling your walk with God, these features help you stay engaged, organized, and growing in your faith.

Track Your Journey

Build consistency with a reading calendar and progress tracker that helps you stay on course. Whether you're following a plan or going at your own pace, see how far you've come in your walk through the Word — one day at a time.
