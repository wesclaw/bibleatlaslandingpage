Frequently Asked Questions

Bible Atlas is an app designed to help you explore and connect with the Bible and Christianity on a deeper level. Unlock characters and locations as you read, bringing the stories and their meanings to life.

The app is free to download, and you can unlock all characters and locations for free. For access to additional premium features and tools, you can upgrade to our premium plan for $10 a month.

Currently, all features require an active internet connection to provide the most accurate and up-to-date maps and content.

We are always adding new characters, nature sounds, and locations to the app every week.
